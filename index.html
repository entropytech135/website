About us

We’re not here to follow trends — we’re here to create staples that last. Our oversized t-shirts, sweatshirts, and hoodies are made with comfort, simplicity, and real-life wear in mind. Designed with clean cuts, soft fabrics, and relaxed fits, they’re pieces you’ll reach for every day. We keep things intentional — small batch production, timeless tones, and details that feel effortless. No loud logos, no fast fashion — just thoughtfully made essentials for people who value comfort, confidence, and authenticity in what they wear.
